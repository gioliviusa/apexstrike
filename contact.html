Common Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed in 2-3 weeks from start to finish. Complex projects may take 4-6 weeks. We'll give you an exact timeline during your free consultation.

Do I need to be tech-savvy?

Not at all! We handle all the technical stuff. You just tell us what you want your business to achieve, and we'll make it happen. We explain everything in plain English.

What if I already have a website?

Perfect! We can either redesign it, fix what's not working, or help you improve it. We'll start with a free audit to show you what needs attention.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We understand cash flow is important for small businesses. We offer flexible payment options to make it easier to get started.

Will I be able to update my website myself?

Absolutely. We build websites that are easy for you to update, and we'll teach you how. Or, if you prefer, we can handle updates for you.

What happens after my website launches?

We don't disappear! We offer ongoing support and maintenance plans to keep your site secure, fast, and up-to-date. You'll always have someone to call.
